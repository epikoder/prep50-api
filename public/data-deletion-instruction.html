Data Deletion Instructions for Prep50

Last Updated: November 27, 2023

1. Facebook Account Deletion:

a. Accessing Facebook Settings: Log in to your Facebook account associated with Prep50. Navigate to "Settings" by clicking the downward arrow at the top right corner of the Facebook homepage.

b. Deactivating Prep50's Permissions: Select "Apps and Websites" from the menu on the left-hand side. Locate and click on "Prep50" from the list of apps. Click on "Remove" to revoke Prep50's access to your Facebook account.

2. Requesting Data Deletion from Prep50:

a. Contacting Support: Send an email to support@prep50.ng with the subject line "Data Deletion Request for Prep50." Include your registered email or any identifying information used for the Prep50 account.

b. Verification Process: Our support team will verify your request and may ask for additional information for confirmation purposes.

c. Data Deletion Procedure: Upon verification, your data associated with the Prep50 app will be permanently deleted from our servers within 7 days.

3. Additional Information:

- Please note that some residual data may remain in system backups for a limited period as part of our regular backup processes.

- Data deletion is irreversible and will result in the loss of account information, progress, and any personalized settings associated with your Prep50 account.

4. Further Assistance:

For any queries or assistance regarding data deletion or any other concerns, please reach out to our support team at support@prep50.ng.
